Mixed Berry Protein Overnight Oats

Description

A quick and easy recipe for a high protein breakfast to go! Make in bulk, and chill to preserve

Ingredients and Steps (Yields 1 bowl)

  • 25g old fashioned oats
  • 120g plain nonfat greek yogurt
  • 60 ml nonfat milk
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla creme stevia
  • 35g straberries, diced
  • 35g blueberries
  1. Add all of the ingredients to a glass jar or tupperware and stir until combined. Cover and chill for 8 hours
